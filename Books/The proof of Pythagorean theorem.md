## method 2, scale of area
$$
area\_c = \lambda c^2
$$
$$
area\_a = \lambda a^2
$$
$$
area\_b = \lambda b^2
$$


$$
\lambda c^2 = \lambda a^2 + \lambda b^2 \Rightarrow c^2 = a^2 + b^2
